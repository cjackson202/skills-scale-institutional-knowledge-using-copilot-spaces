# Role Onboarding Checklist

This document provides onboarding checklists for team members joining OctoAcme in various roles. Use these checklists to ensure a smooth start and clear understanding of responsibilities.

---

## Common Onboarding Tasks (All Roles)

- [ ] Review [octoacme-project-management-overview.md](octoacme-project-management-overview.md) to understand our project management approach
- [ ] Read your role description in [octoacme-roles-and-personas.md](octoacme-roles-and-personas.md)
- [ ] Review [sample-collab-scenarios.md](sample-collab-scenarios.md) to see how roles interact
- [ ] Get access to required tools and systems (GitHub, Slack, project boards, etc.)
- [ ] Meet your team members and key stakeholders
- [ ] Review current projects and priorities with your manager
- [ ] Understand team communication channels and schedules (standups, planning, retrospectives)
- [ ] Set up your development environment (if applicable)

- [ ] Complete common onboarding tasks above

## Tips for a Successful Onboarding

1. **Ask Questions**: No question is too small. Clarify early to avoid confusion later.
2. **Take Notes**: Document learnings, processes, and contacts for easy reference.
3. **Build Relationships**: Connect with team members across roles and teams.
4. **Seek Feedback**: Regularly check in with your manager and peers on your progress.
5. **Contribute Early**: Start small but contribute meaningfully to build confidence.
6. **Learn the Culture**: Understand team norms, communication styles, and decision-making processes.
7. **Be Patient**: Onboarding takes time. Give yourself space to learn and grow.
